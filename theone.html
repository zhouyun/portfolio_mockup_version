TheOne

Exploring the note-taking experience on digital devices

Design concept

  • Defining the design problem

    1. On a phone, reading is great, taking notes is okay, but reviewing notes can be painful.

    You read a book on your phone, find something inspiring, and then you highlight it. Several days later, you want to cite it but can’t remember in where to find it.

  • 2. Things that you learn and experience are in pieces.

    You take notes on Evernote while you read book in iBook. You tweet links while you read articles on Medium. You write in your notebook placed next to your pillow.

  • Your writing is you,
    and it should be easy and constant.

    People take notes to review and digest. People read, get inspired, take notes and write, rethink, learn, correct, reflect on, and get mature step by step.

    This is a design concept to make notes as an independent project. It keeps track of your inspiration, knowledge, things and questions by date and by source. It’s a reflection of your mindset. It’s the basis to know yourself better.

    TheOne collects notes as a timeline by default. It can also catagoize by source. It synchonizes between different devices.s
