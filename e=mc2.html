Design-it-yourself maze

Individual project, December 2013

  • Design makes a better world, first, my world.

    As a designer, I design something for just myself from time to time. If other people like to too, jackpot!

    I gave the maze to a friend as a gift. I enjoyed creating and doubled the enjoyment by sharing it.

  • I love mazes.

    Do you remember in Inception when Cobb asked Ariadne to design him a maze in two minutes? No way can you solve it so quickly. I have to admit that I am the kind of person who paused the movie just to try to solve the maze rather than to grab more ice cream. You can imagine how excited I am when I decided to make my own maze.

    Image from Inception

  • Sustainability is more fun.

    To share the joy of maze design, I created my maze with movable pieces. People were invited to move the pieces to challenge the next player. People loved solving the current maze and then trying to make it harder for the next person.

  • Ending like this? Not enough.

    I made a 2.5-dimensional maze from acrylic. Despite the wires and controller, it was more about the chaos of the shapes. Watching people interacting with it was great fun.
