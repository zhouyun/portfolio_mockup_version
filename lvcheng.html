UX/UI design and strategy | January 2015-present



    SmartTraveller devotes in building platforms for both tour guides and travelers. I joined SmartTraveller as the first designer and hired key talent for the design team. Our design team worked closely with marketing department. I participated in product strategy and supported product promotion plans.

    Our first website released last March and got really good traffic, but only one out of ten checked our customized service. Based on our google analytic and users’ feedback, we separate our website to a forum for young users to share their journey tips and our customized service for senior users in different styles.

    Guess which is for whom?

    We promoted the forum on Weibo and Wechat to grow our community, and promoted our customized service on local media for more influential cooperation. The strategy worked well.

  • How to get users to our products?

    We promoted products on different platforms, and users scaned QR code to add personal assistant on Wechat. We got 8 times more clients through Wechat than phone call.
